About Me

For the last eight years, I have been working as a social worker, volunteering for different non-profits, and sometimes working as a musician. It brought together two of my favorite activities: helping people and learning stuff. I am currently pursuing a Masters in Public Health from George Washington University and also in a web development bootcamp at General Assembly. I hope to help more people and make new friends with my new skills, perhaps through open source projects. In my free time, I enjoy hiking with my boyfriend and my dog, singing and playing music, coordinating The Austin Esperanto Club, and learning to play Naruto from my son.
